Spinning

An indoor group cycling class to upbeat music. Our spinning classes focus on either speed or endurance. Great for weight loss, as one single class can burn up to 1000 kcal! It also increases cardiovascular fitness. Spinning is brilliant for those looking to work up a sweat at their own pace – you can literally make the class as easy or as difficult as you want. It is also great for those with joint problems as it is relatively low impact.

HIIT

HIIT or high-intensity interval training, is a really time efficient workout in which you give 100% effort for short periods of time, followed by short recovery periods. The training sessions follow a circuit taking in a range of bodyweight, light-weight and cardio exercises. Benefits: You'll burn more fat, become stronger and improve your agility and speed. Your heart will also become more efficient.

Yoga

There is good reason that yoga has become an integral part of top athlete’s training programmes. From rugby players to Ireland’s top hurlers, they are all at it. In essence, yoga involves a series of poses and stretches which help you to gain strength and improve flexibility. Benefits: Increased flexibility and muscle strength, which leads to injury prevention. Yoga is also great for the mind!

Boxing

Our gym-based boxing workout includes pad work, bag work and some boxing-specific strength and conditioning routines. Benefits: Burns fat fast and improves cardiovascular fitness as well as core strength. You'll also become stronger and more explosive. Furthermore, boxing is a great way to relieve stress.

Abs Class

Our Abs Class is a great way of building core strength and core stability. The class is short and painful, taking in a wide range of core exercises from sit-ups to plank and everything in between. Benefits: A strong core helps prevent injury, improves posture and eliminates back pain.
